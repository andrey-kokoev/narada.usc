# Real Estate Question Map

## Principal Authority

- Is the focus residential, commercial, or mixed?
- What is the primary business model? (sales, rentals, property management, mixed)
- What roles exist? (owner, agent, property manager, tenant, applicant)
- Are lease execution and payment collection in scope?
- What compliance or jurisdiction constraints apply?

## Semantic Authority

- Is maintenance request tracking required?
- What payment methods must be supported?
- Are MLS or accounting integrations required?

## Planning Authority

- What is the target property or unit count for MVP?
- What is the geographic scope for initial launch?
