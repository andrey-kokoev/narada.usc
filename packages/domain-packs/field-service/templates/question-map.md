# Field Service Question Map

## Principal Authority

- What types of service work are in scope? (preventive, corrective, installation, inspection, etc.)
- How are technicians assigned to work? (manual dispatch, automated scheduling, territory-based, skill-matching)
- Do technicians need mobile access with offline capability?
- What defines work order completion? (signature, photo, form, parts used, customer approval)
- Is billing time-and-materials, flat rate, or contract-based?

## Semantic Authority

- Are parts/inventory tracked per technician or centralized?
- What SLA or priority tiers apply?
- What CRM or ERP integrations are required?

## Planning Authority

- What is the target geography for initial rollout?
- What is the technician capacity and growth expectation?
