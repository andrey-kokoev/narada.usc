# Customer Portal Question Map

## Principal Authority

- What customer identity and authentication model is required?
- What account and profile management can customers perform?
- What documents and files should customers access or upload?
- What support request capabilities are needed?
- What billing visibility is required?
- What notification types and channels are needed?
- Can customers invite or manage delegated users?
- What branding and localization requirements apply?
- What is the MVP self-service boundary?

## Semantic Authority

- What internal systems must the portal integrate with?
- What are the API contracts and failure semantics for integrations?
- What data privacy and consent management requirements apply?

## Planning Authority

- What is the target customer volume and geography?
- What compliance requirements apply to customer data?
