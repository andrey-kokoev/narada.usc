# Notifications and Messaging Question Map

## Principal Authority

- What channels are required? (email, SMS, push, in-app, webhook, chat)
- What message types are in scope? (transactional, promotional, alert, digest)
- How is consent managed? (explicit opt-in, implicit, granular per-channel)
- Is scheduling or throttling required? (immediate only, scheduled, rate-limited)
- What compliance rules apply? (CAN-SPAM, GDPR, TCPA, none)

## Semantic Authority

- What delivery guarantee is required? (at-most-once, at-least-once, exactly-once)
- What provider integrations are preferred?

## Planning Authority

- Are open/click tracking or engagement analytics required?
- What is the expected message volume?
