# Data Pipeline Question Map

## Principal Authority

- What are the source systems and their data formats?
- What ingestion mode is required?
- What data quality and validation rules apply?
- What freshness/SLA is required for each data product?
- What are the retention and privacy requirements?

## Semantic Authority

- What schema and evolution policy is required?
- How should backfills and reprocessing be handled?
- What monitoring and alerting is required?
