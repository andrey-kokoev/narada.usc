# Analytics Dashboard Question Map

## Principal Authority

- What metrics and KPIs must be tracked?
- What are the data sources and their schemas?
- What dimensions and filters are required?
- What refresh cadence is required?
- Who can access which dashboards and metrics?
- What export and sharing capabilities are required?
- What alerting thresholds and channels are needed?
- Is this dashboard-only, embedded, or self-service analytics?
- What is the MVP dashboard set and who is the audience?

## Semantic Authority

- How much historical data must be backfilled?
- What data quality and lineage requirements apply?
- What are the source system schemas and update frequencies?

## Planning Authority

- What infrastructure is available for data processing?
- What is the target performance for query response times?
