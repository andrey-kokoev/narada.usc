# Billing & Subscriptions Question Map

## Principal Authority

- What pricing model is required?
- What is the plan/package structure and how are entitlements mapped?
- What subscription states and transitions are required?
- Are trials, promotions, or discounts required?
- What usage metrics must be metered and how are they aggregated?
- What tax obligations (VAT/GST/sales tax) apply and where?
- What payment methods must be supported and who processes them?
- What is the dunning and retry policy for failed payments?
- Who has authority to issue refunds and credits?
- What revenue recognition rules and reporting are required?

## Semantic Authority

- What integrations with accounting, CRM, product catalog, and usage sources are needed?
- What payment failure handling semantics are required?

## Planning Authority

- What is the target geography for billing?
- What compliance requirements apply to payment data?
