# Internal Tools Question Map

## Principal Authority

- Who are the users and what are their roles?
- What entities and CRUD surfaces are required?
- What actions require approval and who approves?
- What import/export capabilities are needed?
- What audit trail is required?
- What is the blast radius of destructive admin actions?

## Semantic Authority

- What is the source of truth for each entity?
- What reporting needs and data sources exist?
