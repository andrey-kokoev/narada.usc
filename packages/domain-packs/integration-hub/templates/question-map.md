# Integration Hub Question Map

## Principal Authority

- What are the source and target systems?
- What is the sync direction?
- How are identities mapped and resolved between systems?
- What conflict resolution strategy is required?
- What delivery guarantee is required?
- What reconciliation strategy and cadence are needed?
- What observability is required for sync health?
- What backfill or replay scope is required?

## Semantic Authority

- What schema transformations and validations are required?
- What rate limits and quotas must be respected?
- How are credentials and secrets managed?
- What failure semantics are required?

## Planning Authority

- What infrastructure is available for sync orchestration?
- What is the expected data volume and throughput?
