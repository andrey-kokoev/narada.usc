# Healthcare Operations Question Map

## Principal Authority

- Who is the primary user? (patient, provider, staff, administrator)
- What care workflows are in scope? (appointments, referrals, intake, reminders, records)
- How are appointments scheduled? (patient self-service, staff-only, walk-in, mixed)
- What compliance framework applies? (HIPAA, state, institutional, none)
- What is the clinical vs administrative boundary?
- Is billing or insurance coordination in scope?

## Semantic Authority

- Are EHR/EMR or lab integrations required?
- What consent and privacy model is required?

## Planning Authority

- What is the expected patient or client volume?
- What is the rollout strategy by department or location?
