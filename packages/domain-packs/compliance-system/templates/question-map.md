# Compliance System Question Map

## Principal Authority

- What compliance frameworks and standards are in scope?
- What is the controls and evidence model?
- What review and approval workflows are required?
- What is the retention policy for evidence and audit records?
- What access controls and segregation of duties are required?
- How are exceptions and risk acceptance tracked?
- What reporting and export requirements do auditors/regulators need?
- Who owns attestation and what is the accountability model?
- What is the monitoring cadence?
- What is the scope boundary for compliance coverage?

## Semantic Authority

- What are the evidence collection sources and automation level?
- What are the data formats and schemas for evidence?
- How is evidence integrity and immutability ensured?

## Planning Authority

- What is the timeline for audit readiness?
- What resources are available for control implementation?
