# SaaS Question Map

## Principal Authority

- Who is the target customer? B2B, B2C, or vertical-specific?
- What tenant isolation model is required?
- What billing model is expected?
- What compliance requirements apply?
- Is onboarding self-serve, sales-led, or hybrid?

## Semantic Authority

- What auth methods must be supported?
- What data isolation guarantees are required between tenants?

## Planning Authority

- What cloud/hosting model is preferred?
- What is the target geography for initial launch?
