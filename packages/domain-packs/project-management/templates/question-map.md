# Project Management Question Map

## Principal Authority

- What is the primary planning model? (sprint, kanban, milestone, waterfall, hybrid)
- What entities exist? (tasks, projects, epics, portfolios, milestones)
- How are items assigned? (individual, team, role, auto-assigned)
- What defines done? (status, approval, checklist, review)
- Are estimates required? (time, points, t-shirt, none)

## Semantic Authority

- What permission model is needed? (open, project-private, org-private, role-based)
- Are code repo, docs, or chat integrations required?

## Planning Authority

- What reporting is required at MVP?
- What is the expected project and user scale?
